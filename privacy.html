Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026  ·  CapeLoad Logistics (Pty) Ltd, Cape Town, South Africa

This is a draft privacy policy currently in effect. A legally reviewed version will be published soon. By using CapeLoad, you acknowledge this policy.

1. Who We Are

CapeLoad Logistics (Pty) Ltd ("CapeLoad", "we", "us") operates an online logistics marketplace connecting clients and owner-operator drivers in South Africa. Our registered address is in Cape Town, South Africa. You can contact us at info@capeload.co.za.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Full name, email address, phone number, and password (hashed) when you register.
  • Driver information: South African driving licence number, vehicle registration, make, model, and year, as well as banking details for payment processing.
  • Booking information: Pickup and drop-off addresses, cargo description, selected vehicle type, date, time, and payment method.
  • Usage data: Pages visited, actions taken on the platform, browser type, and IP address for security and analytics.
  • Newsletter: Email address if you subscribe to promotional updates.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To process bookings and connect clients with drivers.
  • To send transactional emails (booking confirmations, status updates, account notifications).
  • To send promotional emails to newsletter subscribers (you may unsubscribe at any time).
  • To process driver payments and calculate commissions.
  • To improve the platform and detect fraudulent activity.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only as necessary:

  • Between clients and drivers: Contact details and job information are shared to facilitate completed bookings.
  • Service providers: We use Supabase (database and authentication), Brevo (email delivery), and Yoco (payment processing). These providers process data on our behalf under their own privacy policies.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by South African law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure with industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. We implement row-level security to ensure users can only access their own data. Despite our efforts, no system is 100% secure — please use a strong, unique password for your account.

6. Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active, or as required to provide services and comply with legal obligations. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by emailing us at info@capeload.co.za.

7. Your Rights (POPIA)

Under South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of your personal information.
  • Object to the processing of your data for direct marketing.
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Regulator (South Africa).

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@capeload.co.za.

8. Cookies

CapeLoad uses minimal browser storage (localStorage) to maintain your login session. We do not use third-party advertising cookies. Basic analytics may be added in future, with notice provided at that time.

9. Third-Party Links

Our platform may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies.

10. Children

CapeLoad is not intended for use by persons under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify registered users by email or by displaying a notice on the platform. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact Us

For privacy-related queries or to exercise your rights, contact us at info@capeload.co.za or via WhatsApp at 062 569 2939.
